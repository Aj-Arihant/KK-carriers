Services

Import and Export services

We provide complete end to end services in both import and export consignment via sea , air and road. More than anything we our known for services, we believe in saving as much time as possible for our clients and hence benifitting them in the long run.

IMPORT CONSIGNMENT

  • Study of the different items of import and their correct Classification under the Customs Tariff and Import Trade Control regimes prevailing
  • Maximisation to the extent possible of the Customs duty and ITC benefits to the importers through a judicious application of the laws and regulation
  • Providing quick and timely Customs clearance and delivery services to importers, with special emphasis on avoidance of unwarranted expenses such as demurrage, container detention, etc.
  • Providing all additional services such as DEPB/DFRC Verification, DEECaudit services etc as per the specific requirements of importers.
  • Arrangement of fast and reliable transportation of imported goods from the port of entry to importer's designated site, if required.
  • Advising/acting on any other inputs on services that may be required by the clients

    • EXPORT CONSIGNMENT

  • Filing and Passing of shipping bill immediately on receipt of documents
  • Arrangement of container, transportation and documentation for house stuffing, where desired by the client
  • No-delay carting and examination of goods / seal check of factory stuffed containers
  • Issue of Bill of Lading at the very earliest possible. Of particular importance in handling export shipments is the Bill of Lading/Airway Bill, as late receipt would cause interest and revenue loss to the shipper. It is always our endeavour to expedite clearance of export documents to ensure that there is no delay in issue of the Bill of Lading. This instant turnaround of export documents is the mark of our service to exporters
  • Completion of DEPB/DFRC/DEEC/Drawbackformalities as desired by the exporters
  • Optimising freight charges through our excellent contacts with the shipping industry
